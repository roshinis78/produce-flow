The Danger Zone

What is the Danger Zone? As explored in the worldview, many of our top producers for a given produce are also its top exporters. Sure, this may be wonderful for the economy in the short-term. However, in the long-term, countries who export a majority of the crops that they produce are doing so at the cost of deforestation, intensive water use, and cheap labor.

To investigate these potential environmental hazards, use the interactive visualization below to identify crops that have a shockingly low percent consumption. Percent consumption refers to the percentage of a crop that is actually consumed by a country (the percent of the crop yield that is not exported).
