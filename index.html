Welcome to Produce Flow!

This project was built by Roshini Saravanakumar, in collaboration with Rajesh Mohan.

Produce Flow refers to the global flow of produce across countries and regions and how these flows may expose unsustainable growing or unethical labor practices.

Phase 1 (Worldview and The Danger Zone) explores the patterns in production and exportation of crops worldwide and defines a basic measure of percent consumption to identify potential environmental hazards that result from producing a crop solely for exportation.

Phase 2 (Coming Soon!) will attempt to deterministically label environmental hazards and their underlying causes. These countries and/or regions will be marked for investigation with a suggested course of action to prevent the hazard from causing irreversible changes to our planet.
